## Summary of must-fix (Critical/High) — grouped

1. **Semantics & landmarks** — add a `<section>` with heading and convert items to `<ul>/<li>`; promote `agentName` to a real heading.
2. **Status conveyance** — stop relying on color alone; add icons + `aria-label`/`role="status"`; fix white-on-red/blue contrast.
3. **Live announcements** — add an `aria-live` region for new/updated steps and for state changes.
4. **Truncated output** — drop the `title`-only pattern; use `aria-label` with full text or CSS-only ellipsis + expand control; avoid the `"..."` screen-reader trap.
5. **Empty state** — improve contrast and announce via `role="status"`.

Once those are addressed, the component will be navigable, perceivable, and operable for keyboard-only, screen-reader, and high-contrast users, and the color-only status pattern will be eliminated.
