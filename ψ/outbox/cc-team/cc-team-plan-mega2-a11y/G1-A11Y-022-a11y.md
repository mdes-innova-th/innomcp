| Critical | Chat Item (`<div onClick>`) | **Keyboard Trap / Non-interactive element**: Using a `<div>` for an interactive item makes it unreachable via keyboard (no `tabIndex`) and lacks a semantic role. | High | Action Buttons (Rename/Delete) | **Missing Accessible Name / Emoji Trap**: Emojis (`✏️`, `🗑️`) are used as icons. Screen readers may read them unpredictably (e.g., "memo", "wastebasket").
