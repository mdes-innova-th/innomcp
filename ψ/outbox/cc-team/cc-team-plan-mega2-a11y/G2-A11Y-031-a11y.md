- **The `theme` prop** is unused; consider removing it or using it to apply high‑contrast theme variations.
- **Test with actual screen readers** (NVDA, VoiceOver, JAWS) to catch any remaining traps or missing announcements.

The most critical issues are the lack of accessible names on icon‑only buttons and the non‑interactive list items, which together make the sidebar functionally inaccessible to keyboard and screen reader users.
