## Design Context

### Users
Thai government and enterprise employees — primarily MDES (Ministry of Digital Economy and Society) staff.  
Use context: desktop, during office hours, in a standard workplace environment.  
Job to be done: query AI for weather, laws/regulations, geo data, calculations, and general knowledge work.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **precise, trustworthy, capable**  
Emotional goal: confidence and clarity — users should feel supported, not overwhelmed.  
Tone: professional Thai government digital service; modern but not flashy.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode (office daylight context)  
- **Visual tone**: Refined institutional minimalism — clean whitespace, purposeful hierarchy, subtle teal-green accents  
- **Anti-references**: No neon-on-dark, no AI-purple gradients, no playful consumer-chat aesthetics  
- **Reference feel**: Linear / Notion meets Thai e-Government service portal — efficient, legible, trustworthy  

### Design Principles
1. **Purposeful restraint** — every element earns its place; no decorative noise  
2. **Thai-first legibility** — Sarabun at sufficient weight/size; Thai text needs slightly looser leading  
3. **Contextual affordances** — empty states teach; collapsed views retain utility  
4. **Color as signal, not decoration** — teal/green primary used sparingly for interactive focus; not splashed everywhere  
5. **Transitions, not animations** — state changes feel smooth and snappy; no bouncy or elastic effects
